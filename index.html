About Community Development Fund

Constituency Development Fund (CDF) plays a major role in the improvement of the economic standards of the constituency and the country in general. The purpose of this proposal is to investigate a manually operated system and look into ways of computerizing it. Computerized systems enhance speed and efficiency. They also improve services rendered in terms of accuracy and time. There are a number of ways of ensuring that the system is improved and this can be achieved through computerizing the system completely or partially computerizing it. This project aims at developing a system that allows users to apply for allocation of CDF funding, tenders ,check for project?s progress.
